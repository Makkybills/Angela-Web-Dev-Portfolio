REEL MAKKY

My Top 3 favourite movies of all time.

These are three movies I can watch repeatedly without getting bored. Each one stands out for different reasons, from action-packed scenes to unforgettable comedy moments. So let's dive in them right now.

1. Salaar 1 - Ceasefire

I love Salaar a whole lot. First, because Prabhas is one of my favourite Indian actor and most of his movies are really good. This is a high-octane action film that follows Deva, a fierce and loyal warrior who is drawn into a brutal conflict to protect his childhood friend. Set in the powerful and dangerous kingdom of Khansaar, the movie combines intense action sequences, political intrigue and strong themes of friendship and loyalty. Its stunning visuals and gripping storyline makes it an exciting watch for action lovers like myself. I can't wait for the season 2 to come out soon.

2. Tale of the nine-tailed Season 1 & 2

This is a south-korean fantansy drama. This was where I fell in love with Lee Dong Wook who played Lee Yeon in the movie. The drama is centered on a mythical nine-tailed fox who lives among humans while protecting the world from supernatural threats, as he waits for his the reincarnation of his love who died 600 years ago. The series blends fantasy, romance, action, comedy and folklore, creating a captivating and emotional story.The movie was so so so so so so good. Infact, this movie is my all time favourite K-drama movie.Season 2 was the icing on the cake. I am more in love with the S2 than I am with S1

3. Rush hour 1-3

God bless whoever came up with the Chris Tucker and Jackie Chan combo. If you are looking for a good movie to unwind or to relax or you are extremely bored, this is your go-to movie. The movie follows Detective James Carter and Inspector Lee as they team up to solve international crimes. The combination of Jackie Chan's martial arts skills and Chris Tucker's energetic humor makes the series one of thest entertaining action-comedy franchises ever made.
